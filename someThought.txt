-> click every side once and the mid

2. if a corner and adjacent are the same color and the rest is uniform
-> click on the side and corner which is not the second color

3. if just one side differs
-> click on a adjacent corner and do 2

4. if just a corner differs
-> click a "adjacent" corner and the missing side  and do 2

5. if only the mid differs
-> click on each corner and the mid once maybe repeat this step
